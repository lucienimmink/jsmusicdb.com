JSMusicDB

JSMusicDB, the AudioStation alternative for Synology nas systems, which offers:

  • Gapless playback
  • Multi codec support
  • Playlist support, storing the playlists in AudioStation format so they can be opened in AudioStation as well.
  • Insanely fast; using a 160GB+ audio collection only takes a few seconds to load, and is instantly to use.
  • Full last.fm support; did you know that all your liked tracks that we could find on your nas are grouped in a special playlist?
  • Generate a playlist based on your favorite artist or by selecting 50 random songs; ah but of course you can save those playlists; and play them with AudioStation if you want to.
  • Updated on a regular basis, adding more features; eliminating bugs
