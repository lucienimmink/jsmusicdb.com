LinMusicDB

LinMusicDB, the linux music player:

  • Connects to JSMusicDB
  • Syncing playlists with JSMusicDB/AudioStation
  • Playing all computer stored music.
  • IntelliPlay® knows when to play music from the computer or when to fetch them from your NAS.
  • Can play music videos from YouTube instead of listening to the stored audio.
  • Minimizes to the notification tray and shows popups when a new track start.
  • Scans your music in the background using python. This stores a cache file in the folder so the next time all your music is available in a few seconds.
  • Has a 'mini' mode with the emphasis on the album art with a non-intrusive spectrum analyser that senses the dominant colour of the album art.
  • Notifies you when a new version is available.
